Meet Christi

Christi Stone is a South Central Pennsylvania Photographer with 7 years experience! Offering *All inclusive, natural photography focusing on honest documentation of your life stories.

As a momma of 4 incredible tiny humans, I absolutely hoard photos of them... of all my loved ones, really. Time is of the essence, we won't get these special + precious moments back, It's so important to hold on to these memories + I want to give you them to hold on to forever. All of it... every giggle, smile, kiss, silly face, and even those tears... every image we take will go home with you, where they belong.
